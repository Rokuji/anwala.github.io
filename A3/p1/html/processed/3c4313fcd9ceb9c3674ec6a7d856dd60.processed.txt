Streamed live on Jan 11, 2017The Africa Cup of Nations (AFCON) will soon be in full swing. The tournament, which features 16 teams and will last until February 5th  will taking place in Gabon, initially it was set to be hosted by Libya. However, Confederation of African Football rescinded their right to host the competition due to ongoing war in the country. Sixteen teams, four groups of four will be fighting it out to become champions of Africa, however this tournament has become increasingly well balanced as there is no national team stands out. All of which makes it hard to predict whether Ivory Coast will retain a trophy won so dramatically two years ago; if Egypt can make it eight titles; or whether Ghana or Cameroon can make it five.

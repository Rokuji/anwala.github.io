                                    Al Jazeera English wins Cable & Satellite Channel Of The Year
as well as Network Of The YearFirst time in awards history has one news channel won both awards Al Jazeera English also wins five awards in various
categoriesSteve Chao wins Best Current Affairs PresenterDoha - 2nd December, 2016Al Jazeera English has been crowned Cable & Satellite Channel Of
The Year as well as Network Of The Year at the Asian TV Awards in Singapore. The Channel received the double accolade after winning five awards in various
categories in programmes and online.101 East picked up two awards as cameraman Matt Allard won
Best cinematography for his work for “On Thin Ice: India’s National Ice
Hockey Team” which followed the fortunes of India’s ice hockey team. For
the second consecutive year 101 East presenter Steve Chao picked up Best
Current Affairs Presenter, while Al Jazeera’s Nagieb Khaja received a
commendation for the same category. 101 East also picked up another
commendation for “Too Young to Wed” in the Social Awareness category.Al Jazeera’s Witness picked up two awards for “China
Fake Boyfriends” for Best Social Awareness Programme and Best Direction by
Daniel Holmes. “China’s Fake Boyfriends” tells the story of Li
Chenxi, a single woman who is under pressure to find a partner and settle down.
The programme documents her decision to commit the ultimate deception by hiring
a handsome boyfriend in Beijing to take home to meet her family and friends.“India Menstruation Man” which was directed by
Dirk Gilson and the AJE Digital Video Team won the prize for Best Digital Non
Fiction. The AJE Short digital film told the story of Arunachalam Muruganantham
who was obsessed with making the perfect sanitary pad for his wife. After years
of work, his invention has changed the lives of millions of women in India.“We are delighted and honoured to win the Cable &
Satellite Channel Of The Year as well as Network Of The Year at what is the
most significant and celebrated event in the Asian TV industry. Especially as
this is the first time any news channel has won both awards on the same night”
said acting Managing Director of Al Jazeera English, Giles Trendle. “Al Jazeera
English is an important player in the international news and current affairs
market – and our remit includes bold journalism and covering compelling stories
in the Asia region through our news coverage, documentaries and digital
content. We are thrilled to get such recognition for the important work we do.”Launched in 1996, Asian Television Awards is the Asian TV
industry’s most significant and celebrated event, recognising excellence in

<p style="text-align: center;"><i>Taylor Swift to Headline DIRECTV Super Saturday Night in Houston</i></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><i>Concert in Houston First of Many Events and Experiences for Fans</i></p>
<p><a href="http://about.att.com/category/all_news.html">AT&amp;T</a>* signed a major, exclusive multi-year, multi-faceted deal featuring performances and content with 10-time GRAMMY® award-winning singer, songwriter, musician and producer Taylor Swift.</p>
<p>As part of the deal with AT&amp;T, Taylor Swift will headline DIRECTV Super Saturday Night in Houston the night before the Big Game. This is the 12<sup>th</sup> annual DIRECTV event leading up to the biggest sporting event of the year, and the 1<sup>st</sup> time Taylor Swift will perform during the festivities.</p>
<p>AT&amp;T will share more details on the new multi-year relationship in the near future.</p>
<p>Taylor Swift, the only woman in Grammy history to ever win Album of the Year twice, will provide fans with the ultimate experience Saturday, Feb. 4, at a custom made 64,000 square foot venue in Houston created especially for this event. Fans will have access to tickets through a series of exclusive AT&amp;T promotions and campaigns.&nbsp; &nbsp;</p>
<p>“We’re thrilled to reach a deal to bring Taylor and her unique talents to her fans and our customers in new and exclusive ways,” said John Stankey, CEO, AT&amp;T Entertainment Group. “And we’ve thrilled attendees during the Big Game weekend for the last 11 years with A-list parties and the hottest performing music artists around, so 2017 will be our very best. We’re excited to get event tickets into the hands of Taylor’s fans and exclusive content from the event to our customers and celebrate the combination of one of the world’s biggest artists with one of the world’s biggest sporting events.”</p>
<p>Super Saturday Night in Houston is one of many opportunities AT&amp;T and Taylor Swift will team up for in the coming months.&nbsp;</p>
<p>For more information on the AT&amp;T and Taylor Swift relationship and tickets for the Houston event, follow AT&amp;T and Taylor Nation social media handles.</p>
<p></p>
<p>*AT&amp;T products and services are provided or offered by subsidiaries and affiliates of AT&amp;T Inc. under the AT&amp;T brand and not by AT&amp;T Inc.</p>

<p><b>About Taylor Swift</b></p>
<p>In the fall of 2014, when Taylor Swift released her critically acclaimed fifth album,&nbsp;<b><i>1989</i></b>, she astounded the world by selling almost 1.3 million albums in its debut week -- a feat that had been called impossible. Taylor is the only artist in history to have three albums selling over one million copies in their first week of release (2010's&nbsp;<b><i>Speak Now</i></b><i>,&nbsp;</i>2012's&nbsp;<b><i>RED</i>&nbsp;</b>and 2014's&nbsp;<b><i>1989</i></b>). Taylor, a ten-time GRAMMY winner, is a singer, musician and songwriter, and not only the youngest winner in history, but the <i>only</i> female solo artist to win the music industry's highest honor, the GRAMMY Award for&nbsp;<i>Album of the Year&nbsp;</i>twice. Taylor has an album on Rolling Stone's prestigious&nbsp;<i>The 50 Greatest Albums of All Time</i>&nbsp;(by women) list, she is Billboard's youngest-ever&nbsp;<i>Woman of the Year</i>&nbsp;and the only artist to have been awarded this honor twice, Time magazine has named her one the of the&nbsp;<i>100 Most Influential People</i>&nbsp;in the world and one of only eight candidates for their most prestigious honor, <i>2014 Person of the Year </i>and is the&nbsp;inaugural&nbsp;recipient&nbsp;of AMA's&nbsp;<i>Dick Clark Award for Excellence</i>.&nbsp;The number of awards she has won and the sales figures only tell part of Taylor's story. It’s the intangibles that elevate Taylor into the stratosphere of our pop culture planet, allowing the 26-year old singer-songwriter to orbit in a more rarified air. Her large-scale charitable contributions are one thing, but it’s in the small gestures – the notes of compassion she posts on the Instagram photos of lovelorn fans, the genuine hugs she distributes without discretion – where Swift proves time and time again that platinum-selling, record-setting success has not changed her inherent nature. She is awkwardly honest and powerfully empathetic; a brazen superfan, loyal friend, fierce protector of hearts; and one of the world’s greatest ambassadors for the power of just being yourself.</p>

    <div class="field field-name-field-post-body field-type-text-long field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even"><p><a href="//www.mlssoccer.com/players/kaka">Kaká</a> didn’t waste any time making his mark in his first appearance of 2016, scoring a goal and adding two assists in <a href="//matchcenter.mlssoccer.com/matchcenter/2016-04-03-orlando-city-sc-vs-portland-timbers/recap">Orlando City’s 4-1 rout against the Portland Timbers on Sunday</a>.</p>

<p>On Tuesday, the Brazilian star was recognized for his excellent season debut, when it was announced that he was voted MLS Player of the Week for Week 5 of the&nbsp;season by the North American Soccer Reporters (NASR).</p>

<p>Kaká, who missed Orlando’s first three games of the year due to injury, assisted on the Lions first goal on Sunday by&nbsp;<a href="//matchcenter.mlssoccer.com/matchcenter/2016-04-03-orlando-city-sc-vs-portland-timbers/details/video/61241">curling a perfect free kick into the area</a> that defender <a href="//www.mlssoccer.com/players/sebastian-hines">Seb Hines</a> headed home in the 13th minute. The 33-year-old attacking midfielder&nbsp;notched another helper in the 32nd minute, connecting with defender <a href="//www.mlssoccer.com/players/brek-shea">Brek Shea</a> on a give-and-go before the <a href="//matchcenter.mlssoccer.com/matchcenter/2016-04-03-orlando-city-sc-vs-portland-timbers/details/video/61255">American international smashed home a&nbsp;volley</a>.</p>

<p>He put the game out of reach minutes into the second half, <a href="//matchcenter.mlssoccer.com/matchcenter/2016-04-03-orlando-city-sc-vs-portland-timbers/details/video/61271">recovering a loose ball at the top of the box and slotting home</a> past Portland goalkeeper <a href="//www.mlssoccer.com/players/adam-kwarasey">Adam Kwarasey</a> in the 48th minute to give Orlando a 3-0 lead.</p>

<p>Kaká and Orlando, who sit atop the Eastern Conference standings with a 2-0-2 record, will return to action on Friday&nbsp;when they take on&nbsp;the Philadelphia Union at Talen Energy Park (7 pm ET; UniMas in the US, MLS LIVE in Canada). &nbsp;</p>

<p>The MLS Player of the Week is selected each week of the regular season by a panel of journalists from the NASR. The group consists of members of print, television, radio and online media.</p>

<h4 class="panel-title"><a href="#collapse2" data-toggle="collapse" data-parent="#accordion">Treating Insomnia</a></h4>

<div id="collapse2" class="panel-collapse collapse">
<div class="panel-body">
<p>The Fisher Wallace Stimulator® treats insomnia by stimulating the brain to produce serotonin and melatonin while reducing cortisol (the stress hormone) and calming the brain's Default Mode Network. The device is effective in treating the following types of insomnia:</p>
<ul style="margin: 20px 0; padding: 0;">
<li style="list-style: none; margin: 5px 0;">
<b>Chronic Insomnia</b>: Difficulty falling asleep or staying asleep three or more nights per week.</li>
<li style="list-style: none; margin: 5px 0;">
<b>Onset Insomnia</b>: Difficulty falling asleep at the beginning of the night.</li>
<li style="list-style: none; margin: 5px 0;">
<b>Comorbid Insomnia</b>: Insomnia that occurs in conjunction with depression or anxiety.</li>
<li style="list-style: none; margin: 5px 0;">
<b>Maintenance Insomnia</b>: Difficulty staying asleep through the night (waking up often or waking up too early).</li>
<li style="list-style: none; margin: 5px 0;">
<b>Acute Insomnia</b>: Temporary difficulty falling asleep or staying asleep as the result of traveling or a life event.</li>
</ul>
